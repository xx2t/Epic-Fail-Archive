About Epic Fail

​Epic Fail Terms:

I thought it would be fun to say a few of the terms I use (mostly in my mind).  Most of them come from how I draw/ organize things.  So, here they are!
​
STRIP: A two frame strip, what I post 3 times a week.
PAGE: 10 strips, based on the way I write them down when planning upcoming strips.
STORY: A set of strips which, well, tell a story.
SIDE STORIES: Stories that are not posted on the comic page. (AKA seasonal stories)
CHARACTER PORTFOLIO: A description and mugshot of any one character from Epic Fail or any side stories.
CHARACTER SHEET: A set of 6 Character portfolios.
MAIN CHARACTER PORTFOLIO: A character portfolio of one of Epic Fail's main characters, such as Joey and George.
SIDE CHARACTER PORTFOLIO: A character portfolio of one of Epic Fail's minor characters, such as Male Newscaster 1 and Interrogator.
SEASONAL CHARACTER PORTFOLIO:  A character portfolio of a character that appears only or mostly in a seasonal story, such as Hillary Clintwood and Donald Hair.
